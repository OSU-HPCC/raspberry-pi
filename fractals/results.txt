Pretty cool right? Even though we choose the dots of the 
triangle randomly we get a triangular fractal pattern 
because we always use the same rule when we take a step.

So far, all of the work has been done by one raspberry pi.
It was fast, but did you notice that it had to think a 
while for 10,000 steps? Let's give it a break and go down
